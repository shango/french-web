Who Le Cahier is, and isn't, for

A good fit if you…
  • ✓ Are starting from zero and want a confident base before grammar
  • ✓ Remember some school French and want to prove it and skip ahead
  • ✓ Want a real finish line, not a subscription that never ends
Not the right tool if you…
  • ✕ Need full grammar and conjugation drills right now
  • ✕ Are already conversational and want speaking practice
  • ✕ Want a game with points and leaderboards to keep you hooked

Not sure? The first words are free, try a session and see if the pace fits. Getting the wrong person to sign up helps no one.
